                              Privacy Policy

                              Effective Date: 1st Jan 2025

                              Introduction

                              At Penny Partner, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using our financial management web application. By using the app, you agree to the practices outlined below.

                              1. Information We Collect

                              We collect the following types of information:

                              • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password to ensure secure access.
                              • Transaction Data: We collect data about your income, expenses, budgets, accounts, and categories to help you manage and analyze your finances.

                              2. How We Use Your Information

                              Your information is used to provide and enhance our services:

                              • Account Authentication: We verify your identity to ensure secure login and protect your account.
                              • Financial Management: We store your transaction data to help you track your finances, set budgets, and generate reports.
                              • Security: We encrypt your password to keep your account secure. If you change your password, you will be logged out of all other devices to protect your information.

                              3. Data Security

                              Your security is our priority:

                              • Password Encryption: We encrypt your password for secure authentication, ensuring only you can access your account.
                              • Transaction Data: Your transaction data is stored securely in its original form to maintain performance while ensuring confidentiality.

                              4. Data Retention

                              We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can delete your account at any time, and we will remove your data from our servers upon request.

                              5. Your Rights

                              You have the right to:

                              • Access and Manage: View and update your personal and transaction information at any time.
                              • Delete: Request the deletion of your account and all associated data.
                              • Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by contacting us.

                              6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

                              We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated to you through the app or on our website. Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the changes.

                              7. Contact Us

                              If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us:

                              • Email: official.devpro@gmail.com
